Welcome To The Curious Thinkers

Who Are We?

We are The Curious Thinkers, Grade 10-2 Students from APEC V.Luna in the Philippines. We were curious on the effectiveness, benefits, and challenges of streaming, So we "The Curious Thinkers" did research.

Purpose of This Website

This website exists to understand the relative effectiveness, benefits, and challenges of streaming. As viewers try to navigate the evolving landscape of streaming, they will have a hard time finding the correct live content type they want. Well, we are here to help by doing research about this topic.

What You Can Expect from this Website

One of the core features of this website is the extensive, in-depth research it provides on live streaming. This research covers a wide array of topics to give visitors a thorough understanding of the strengths and weaknesses of each streaming content or sites. We have also done some data analysis on the audiences of about live podcast, live interview, and informational stream.
